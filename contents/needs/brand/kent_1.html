Brand Position/Statement: KENT is the Expert in Low Tar
STRENGTH
  • Strong positioning in ultra lights segment and perceived by consumers as the "Expert in Low Tar"
  • Segment leader in BVS5 and positioned strongly among BVS5 key brand choice drivers
  • Strong and defendable PoD in menthol (first mover advantage with thread technology)
  • Core brand essence "A Step Ahead/True Pioneer" allows brand to innovate into new segments where competition cannot follow
OPPORTUNITY
  • Perceived amongst SOB as being "too light/ too weak"
  • Does not project any strong image amongst SOB (neither good not bad), preventing top of mind consideration
  • Lack of total brand positioning to link Tastek, Mintek & Premium
  • No tar based line extensions available (no low-hanging fruit)
  • Low ASU30 brand share (lower than national SOM)
  • Uncertainty around future Tastek Upgrade technologies
WEAKNESS
  • Total market trend towards lower tar; BVS5 seen as growing segment
  • Mintek popular among female smokers as alternative to "overtly feminine" brands
  • Concentration of consumers to fewer purchase points post AAVM to create more "even playing field" vs. Mild Seven
  • Support to gain "well known" image making the brand bigger than it is
THREAT
  • Rejuvenation of JT with aggressive moves into Ultra Lights through Mild Seven and Caster
  • Reduced impact of VM to make brand image makeover more difficult and expensive
  • Increasing frequency of excise tax increases potential for VFM segment to develop
  • Lack of Low Smoke technology in the event JT expands D-Spec to Ultra Lights segment
